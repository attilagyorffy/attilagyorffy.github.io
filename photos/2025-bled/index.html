Bled

August 2025. Slovenia is a country of two million people that somehow has a medieval castle on a cliff, glacial water so clear you can count the fish, and blokes rowing tourists around in wooden boats like it’s the 1800s. The whole place looks like someone colour-graded reality and forgot to tell anyone. You walk around the lake, eat a cream cake that has no business being that good, watch the Julian Alps turn pink at sunset, and quietly start googling property prices. 20 frames. Canon EOS 100D.
